Growth funnel and leakage

Funnels compress messy journeys into measurable stages. The leverage point is often mid-funnel—activation, time-to-value, or proof milestones—not only top-of-funnel spend.

How to stage honestly
  • Define entry criteria per stage (intent, fit, technical readiness).
  • Use cohorts by acquisition source; blended averages hide channel-specific breakage.
  • Instrument time between stages; long gaps indicate missing enablement or product friction.
Common traps

Vanity definitions (“lead” = any form fill) and revenue teams optimizing the wrong stage inflate early conversion while starving later proof. Tie stages to economic events where possible.
