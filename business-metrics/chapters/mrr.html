MRR composition

Net MRR movement is the sum of new, expansion, contraction, and churn. Growth quality depends on whether expansion outruns churn—not on new logo hero numbers alone.

Animated bridge in the explorer

The animated explorer sketches one month of MRR movement from sliders—useful for rehearsing sign conventions before you trust a finance export.

Bridge (simplified)
Bucket What moves it
New First-time paying customers in period.
Expansion Seats, usage tiers, cross-sell—same customer pays more.
Contraction Downgrades before churn; often an early warning.
Churn Logo or revenue leaves; track both when usage-based.
Reading the health signal

If new MRR masks weak expansion and rising contraction, efficiency metrics like magic number and payback can still look fine—until they do not. Pair MRR bridges with cohort charts.
