Retention and churn drag

Logo retention and revenue retention can diverge when expansion offsets churn at the account level. Boards care about both: durable usage vs durable dollars.

Cohort hygiene
  • Anchor cohorts on a meaningful start event (paid conversion, first value action).
  • Separate voluntary vs involuntary churn when payment failure dominates.
  • Compare cohorts by ICP tier—enterprise curves rarely look like PLG SMB curves.
Churn drag intuition

Even modest monthly churn compounds into a heavy hole to refill with new bookings. The explorer overlays logo and revenue cohort decay (separate monthly retention sliders), plus a linear cash panel beneath—real curves often show “smile” or “cliff” shapes worth modeling explicitly.
