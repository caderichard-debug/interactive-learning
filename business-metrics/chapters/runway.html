Burn and runway

Runway is how long you can operate at a planned burn before cash runs out. The useful question is not the headline months figure but which bets you can fund before the next financing or breakeven milestone.

Stress dimensions
  • Hiring cadence and lag to productivity.
  • Working capital and collections if you sell annual contracts.
  • Scenario bands on revenue (slippage) and cost (infra + support load).
Operator default

Maintain a rolling 13-week cash view and tie large commits to explicit revenue catalysts. The explorer layers a simple month-by-month cash balance (no inflows) so you can see when linear burn intersects zero—intuition only, not a forecast.
