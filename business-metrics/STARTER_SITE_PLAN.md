# Business Metrics Starter Site Plan

## Intended topics
1. Growth funnel and conversion leakage
2. CAC vs LTV sensitivity
3. Retention cohorts and churn drag
4. MRR composition (new, expansion, contraction, churn)
5. Burn and runway scenarios

## Key points by topic
- **Funnel:** improving one stage can outperform top-of-funnel spend.
- **CAC/LTV:** assumptions about payback period dominate decisions.
- **Retention:** logo churn and revenue churn can move in opposite directions.
- **MRR composition:** growth quality depends on net expansion and churn balance.
- **Runway:** hiring cadence and growth bets should match cash risk tolerance.

## Starter visuals
- Animated funnel bars with adjustable stage conversion rates.
- CAC/LTV gauge and payback panel tied to slider controls.
- Cohort decay sparkline + runway projection panel.
